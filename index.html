About Me

I started my tech career in 2018 with the goal to help a colleague get an online portfolio online with squarespace. A few months after that project, I designed a website to help connect people with each other with Wix. I believe those projects gave me a solid foundation and the motivation to start creating things from scratch. I never really appreciated the power of technology until I started building applications and websites. Now, I genuinely enjoy learning about code and the obstacles that come with it.
