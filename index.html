About Me

I started my career in tech in 2018 with the goal to help a colleague get an online portfolio up and running. A few months after, my friend and I designed a website to help connect people with each other. Although those site never went live, I believe it gave me a solid foundation and motivation to start a career in tech. I never really appreciated the power of technology until I started building applications and websites. Now, I genuinely enjoy learning about code and the obstacles that come with it.
